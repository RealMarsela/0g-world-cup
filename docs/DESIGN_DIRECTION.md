# 0G World Cup Design Direction

- Archetype: Black-red cinematic sports arena with orbital 0G logo language.
- Typography: Bricolage Grotesque for display, Geist for body, Geist Mono for hashes and scores.
- Palette: near-black, hot 0G red, dark crimson panels, trophy gold for winners/proof highlights, muted warm text.
- Density: game cockpit, not a marketing card grid; image-led first viewport with logo/thumbnail/video assets.
- Motion: subtle hover, video atmosphere, and proof emphasis only until browser polish can run.
- Bans: no cursive fonts, no fake deployment badges, no unlabeled simulation claims.
