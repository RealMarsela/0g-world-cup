# 0G World Cup Design Direction

- Archetype: Cyberpunk Neon with live stadium broadcast structure.
- Typography: Bricolage Grotesque for display, Geist for body, Geist Mono for hashes and scores.
- Palette: deep pitch green/black, proof cyan, restrained blue secondary light.
- Density: game cockpit, not a marketing card grid.
- Motion: subtle hover and proof emphasis only until browser polish can run.
- Bans: no cursive fonts, no fake deployment badges, no unlabeled simulation claims.
